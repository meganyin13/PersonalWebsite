Projects

Markets Today -- This winter break I began diving into web development with this new project that unites both my fascination with the stock market and my interest in web design. I developed this project using AngularJS, D3.js, and queries to the CNBC API. I hope to continue this project throughout my high school career--perhaps adding new features to facilitate paper trading and stock price predictions using data science, NLP, and machine learning.

Predicting Synergistic Cancer Drug Combinations -- During high school I interned in the Christina Leslie computational biology lab and worked on project to predict synergistic cancer drug combinations for use in a novel cancer therapy strategy combination therapy. PIDDrive for FeMaidens -- In my robotics team, one of my biggest projects was developing a PID (proportional integral derivative) drive system that would allow the robot to drive itself when driving straight.
